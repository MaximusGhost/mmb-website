The Program

Riverdale Rising Stars

After moving to Riverdale in 2008, my parents discovered the Riverdale Rising Stars program at the Riverdale Y, and I've been performing there since I was 7. Click above to go to the homepage.

Teen Program

After graduating from the Junior program, I joined the Teen program, putting on all sorts of shows, from All Shook Up to Footloose. I am currently in my penultimate show.

Junior Program

I started off in the Junior Rising Stars, with shows like Shrek, Jr. and Guys and Dolls, Jr. I participated in this program until I graduated into the teen program with my last show being You're a Good Man, Charlie Brown.

Earlier Experience

Browadway Babies

My earliest theatre experience actually came from a group my parents put me in before RRS called Broadway Babies, where we would put on small productions. Though I don't remember much from it, this was my first exposure to acting on a stage and part of what helped me become so passionate about RRS in my later years.
