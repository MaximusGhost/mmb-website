Joining Riverdale Kenshikai Karate

After moving to Riverdale and discovering a lot of programs here (such as the RRS theater program), my parents discovered Riverdale Kenshikai Karate and decided to enroll me there. Though I was initially reluctant to go to classes, they eventually grew on me and I have never stopped training there. I started when I was 4, and have been going ever since. Progressions and Promotions

I quickly became fascinated with karate and quickly started progressing through the ranks. Since I started quite young, I began in the "junior" ranks, but when I was old enough, I moved up to the regular ranks as well. Our belt ranks are as follows: white, blue, yellow, green, brown, black, with "advanced" stripes between each color belt. My promotion certificates are available above, in reverse order.
