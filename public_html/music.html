The Next Level

Competitions/Recitals

After a few years of piano playing, I was introduced to the world of competitions and recitals, which took piano playing (as an art and as a passion) to another level.

Golden Key Music Festival

This was my longest-running music competition, as I performed there for many consecutive years. I won numerous silver and gold medals, one of the diplomas for which can be seen on this page. In summer 2024, I had the opportunity to compete and perform in the international chapter, performing in various historic music halls in Vienna.
